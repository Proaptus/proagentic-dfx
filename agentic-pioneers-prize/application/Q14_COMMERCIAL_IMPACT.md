# Q14: Potential Commercial Impact and UK Benefit

**Word Limit**: 400 words
**Appendix**: None allowed
**Scored**: Yes (1-10 by 3 assessors)

---

## Official Prompt

> **What impact might this project have outside the project team?**

---

## Must Explain

1. **Target customers**, value proposition and evidence from this phase that supports ROI
2. **Route to adoption**: partnerships, pricing, delivery and support model you validated or de-risked
3. **Expected UK benefits** such as productivity, cost reduction, quality, exports or resilience
4. **Competitive positioning** and how the work strengthens UK capabilities and IP

---

## Scoring Criteria Summary

### For 9-10 Score (Target)
- Commercial proposition is clear, strong and well supported
- Evidence of demand or ROI is compelling
- Route to adoption is credible and informed by real partnerships or testing
- UK benefits are clearly articulated, specific and meaningful

### For 7-8 Score
- Commercial case is strong but some areas may lack detail
- Evidence of demand reasonable though not comprehensive
- UK benefits described but less fully developed

### Avoid (3-4 Score Indicators)
- Weak or unrealistic commercial case
- Little supporting evidence
- UK benefits unclear or unconvincing
