# Q11: User and Workflow Fit

**Word Limit**: 400 words
**Appendix**: Optional (PDF, max 10MB, 2 A4 pages)
**Scored**: Yes (1-10 by 3 assessors)

---

## Official Prompt

> **Who will use it, where does it fit in the workflow, and what does it improve?**

---

## Must Explain

1. **The real user context**, roles and tasks you are designing for, and any human-in-the-loop steps
2. **Potential implications** for time, quality, cost, safety or reliability in that workflow
3. **Interoperability** with existing tools, systems, data formats and governance constraints

---

## Scoring Criteria Summary

### For 9-10 Score (Target)
- User context and workflow described with strong domain understanding
- Clear insight into real tasks, constraints, dependencies and organisational practices
- Workflow integration is realistic, coherent and linked to challenge
- Human-in-the-loop roles are identified and well justified
- Interoperability with existing tools and governance is addressed credibly
- Assumptions about adoption are explicit and plausible
- Appendix enhances clarity, if submitted

### Avoid (3-4 Score Indicators)
- Limited or shallow understanding of users, roles or workflows
- Proposed integration into real-world processes is unclear or unrealistic
- Human-in-the-loop needs ignored or poorly justified
- Interoperability constraints not recognised
